**Black** - Product identifier

**<font inherit/inherit;;#f39c12;;inherit>Orange</font> **- Product version (SN prefix)

**<font inherit/inherit;;#999999;;inherit>Gray</font> **- Manufacturing line at the factory

**<font inherit/inherit;;#9b59b6;;inherit>Purple</font> **- Year of production

**<font inherit/inherit;;#2ecc71;;inherit>Green</font> **- Week of production

**<font inherit/inherit;;#e74c3c;;inherit>Red</font> **- Product revision

<font inherit/inherit;;#3498db;;inherit>**Blue**</font> - Unit number unique for every vehicle of a specific model made each week

**Product identifier (**black**)**

Represents a version or edition of a scooter.

**Manufacturing date (**<font inherit/inherit;;#9b59b6;;inherit>purple</font>/<font inherit/inherit;;#2ecc71;;inherit>green</font>**)**

The first two digits are the manufacturing year. (21 for 2021, 22 for 2022 etc.)

The last two digits are the manufacturing week.

It can be replaced with anything as it doesn't have any affect on the scooter.

**Identifier/Unit serial number (**<font inherit/inherit;;#3498db;;inherit>blue</font>**)**

An incremental counter for the number of scooters produced per week, a 3 or 4-digit number.

It can be replaced with anything as it doesn't have any purpose for the scooter.

**Prefix letters (**<font inherit/inherit;;#f39c12;;inherit>orange</font>**)**

**A** - F20\\
**B** - F20D\\
**C** - F30\\
**D** - F30D\\
**E** - F40\\
**G** - F40D\\
**H** - F60\\
**I** - F60D/F60E\\
**J** - F60D/F60E\\
**M** - F60A/F60 Asia\\
**N** - F25

**Manufacturing line (**<font inherit/inherit;;#95a5a6;;inherit>gray</font>**)**

Determines which line at the factory was used to make a particular scooter.
