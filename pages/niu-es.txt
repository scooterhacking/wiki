===== Hacking =====

Hacking is only possible with premade binaries flashed via a special tool made by Niu called the [[https://i.imgur.com/0nxNkQA.png|"Niu H1 Diagnostic Tool"]]. Flashing using third party software is not possible at the moment given a lack of research into the scooters' serial communication and the fact that official updates are downloaded via the Internet, not Bluetooth.
